## How to Use

### Step 1: Open Folder
- เลือก sub-folder ที่ตรงกับสิ่งที่จะทำ
- ดู 3-5 references

### Step 2: Analyze
- สังเกต lighting
- สังเกต color
- สังเกต composition
- สังเกต mood

### Step 3: Apply
- **ไม่ copy** — adapt เท่านั้น
- ใช้ style ไม่ใช่ subject
- ใช้ mood ไม่ใช่ composition เป๊ะ

## Prompt Templates (สำหรับ AI Generate)

### Morning Light (Home)
```
A Japanese apartment morning scene, soft golden sunlight
streaming through sheer white curtains, visible dust motes
in the light beam. Tatami floor, simple wood furniture.
A school uniform hanging near the window. Cup of green tea
on a low table. Ghibli-pastel color palette, soft edges,
warm and peaceful atmosphere. 3-point lighting.
Aspect 16:9. Soft, no harsh shadow.
```

### Noon (School)
```
A Japanese middle school exterior, white 3-story building
with navy blue accents, sakura trees blooming in the yard.
Bright noon light, soft shadows. A few students walking
in the background in standard uniforms. Green grass field.
Ghibli-pastel color palette. Soft edges. Atmospheric.
Aspect 16:9.
```

### Evening (Station)
```
A small Japanese JR train station at golden hour, warm
orange light, long shadows. Crosswalk in foreground with
yellow traffic light. Vending machines glowing softly.
A few salarymen and students walking. Sakura petals
in the air. Ghibli-pastel, not cyberpunk. Warm dominant.
Aspect 16:9.
```

### Night (Shopping)
```
A Tokyo shopping arcade at dusk, mixed warm street lights
and subtle neon signs. Ramen shop with steam from the
kitchen visible. A few people walking with shopping bags.
Arcade with game machines inside. Muted but lively color
palette. Ghibli-pastel, NOT cyberpunk. 3-point lighting.
Aspect 16:9.
```

### Sunset (Shrine)
```
A small Japanese shrine with red torii gate in soft
golden hour light. Wooden bridge over a koi pond with
red and white koi visible. Sakura trees in full bloom,
ginkgo trees nearby. Stone lanterns along the path.
Completely peaceful atmosphere. Ghibli-pastel aesthetic.
Soft, warm, contemplative. 3-point lighting.
Aspect 16:9.
```

### Material Close-up
```
Close-up texture of weathered Japanese wood, light oak,
visible grain pattern, soft warm color. Slight wear
and dust on surface. Subtle normal map detail.
Ghibli-pastel aesthetic. Studio lighting, soft shadow.
Material reference, no scene. Aspect 4:3.
```

### Character Reference
```
A Japanese high school student, boy, second year, age 17.
Standard uniform: white shirt, navy blazer, black slacks,
necktie, school bag. Natural hair, no makeup, no anime
styling. Realistic proportions, head:body = 1:7. Standing
naturally, relaxed pose. Soft warm light. Ghibli-pastel
aesthetic but realistic. Not anime, not stylized cartoony.
Aspect 4:5.
```

## Anti-Reference (ห้ามใช้)

### ❌ ห้ามอ้างอิงภาพแนวนี้

- ❌ Cyberpunk neon Tokyo
- ❌ Anime 2D (cel-shaded)
- ❌ Photorealistic Tokyo
- ❌ Tourist landmark (Sensoji, Tokyo Tower)
- ❌ Snow/winter scene
- ❌ Summer festival (over-busy)
- ❌ Autumn red leaves (เกมเรา spring)
- ❌ Kawaii mascot
- ❌ Horror / supernatural
- ❌ Western clothing
- ❌ Fast food / branded

### ✅ ควรอ้างอิงภาพแนวนี้

- ✅ Ghibli film backgrounds
- ✅ Shinkai films (Your Name, etc.)
- ✅ Real residential Tokyo photos
- ✅ Real Japanese students (uniform photos)
- ✅ Real Japanese food photography
- ✅ Real shrine/temple (subtle, not touristy)
- ✅ Studio Ghibli art books
- ✅ Makoto Shinkai art books

## Workflow

### 1. Collect
- เก็บ 5-10 references ต่อ aspect
- บันทึกใน folder ที่ตรง

### 2. Curate
- เลือก 3-5 ที่ตรงที่สุด
- ทิ้งอันที่ไม่ match

### 3. Annotate
- เขียนหมายเหตุในแต่ละรูป
- "ใช้ mood นี้ แต่ไม่ใช่ subject"
- "สีนี้ดี แต่ต้อง soften"

### 4. Apply
- Generate asset/scene
- Compare with reference
- Iterate
